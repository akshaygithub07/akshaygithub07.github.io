My First Novel

15-03-2022

When I say "my first novel , I don't mean that I wrote one . I just happen to read a few and this one is the first one. It was 7th grade and we had just started to issue books from the school library on our own. I went to the library one fine day and just started looking around. In one of the shelf ,I saw a shiny cover which had a boy with sword who was riding a pegasus around a skyscraper.

It had a pretty good name too. Percy jackson and the Titan's Curse. I was a Michael Jackson fan and maybe that played along in a subconsious levelI(who knows!). The description on the back of the book was also quite intriguing. So I just bought it and that's how my journey of reading the novels started.
